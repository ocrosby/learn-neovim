## Tips for Reading Articles

1. **Verify dates** - Neovim evolves quickly; prefer recent articles
2. **Check for Lua** - Modern Neovim uses Lua, not Vimscript
3. **Test commands** - Try examples in Neovim immediately
4. **Use `:help`** - Verify with official documentation
5. **Adapt, don't copy** - Understand concepts, then build your own config
