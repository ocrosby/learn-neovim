## Tips for Learning from Videos

1. **Watch at 1.5-2x speed** - Most tutorial videos can be sped up
2. **Follow along** - Open Neovim and try commands as you watch
3. **Take notes** - Write down useful commands or concepts
4. **Rewatch sections** - Complex topics often need multiple views
5. **Practice immediately** - Apply what you learned right away

## Note

Video tutorials can become outdated as Neovim evolves. Always:
- Check the video date
- Verify commands with `:help`
- Adapt to current Neovim best practices
- Prefer Lua over Vimscript for modern configs
